Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken parm is for folks who want chicken, but also cheese and pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Toss chicken with egg dredge and breadcrumbs
  3. Top with cheese, bake for 40 mins
  4. Bake until cooked; serve with pasta if desired
